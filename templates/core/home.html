🔐 What is Cyber Security?

👉 Protecting the Digital World from Modern Threats. 🌐🛡️

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, servers, networks, applications, and data from digital attacks. 💻🌐

Today, almost everything is online — banking 🏦, shopping 🛒, communication 📱, education 🎓, and government services 🏛️.

In simple words, it means keeping your digital world safe from hackers, malware, phishing, ransomware, and data breaches. 🛡️

Protecting these systems from hacking and cyber threats such as cyber fraud 💳, unauthorized access data theft 📂, identity theft 👤, and malicious activities 🧑‍💻 is known as Cyber Security. 🔐✨

Real-World Cyber Attacks 🌍⚠️

Cyber attacks are rapidly increasing as technology grows. 💻🌐 Attackers target individuals, businesses, and governments to steal data, demand ransom, or disrupt services.

Examples include phishing 🎣, ransomware 🦠, data breaches 📂, and DoS attacks ⚡, leading to financial loss 💳 and reputational damage 📉.

A major case was the WannaCry attack (2017) 🦠, affecting 200,000+ systems worldwide with an estimated $4 billion loss 💰.

These incidents highlight the critical need for strong cyber security measures. 🔐🛡️
