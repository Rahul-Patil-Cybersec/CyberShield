🔐 What is Cyber Security?

Protecting computers, networks and data from hackers and cyber threats.

Cyber Security protects systems from hacking, malware, ransomware and phishing attacks. It keeps online banking, shopping, and personal information safe.

⚠️ Real-World Cyber Attacks

Understanding modern cyber threats helps prevent future attacks.

Cyber attacks like phishing, ransomware and data breaches cause huge financial damage. The famous WannaCry attack infected more than 200,000 computers worldwide.
